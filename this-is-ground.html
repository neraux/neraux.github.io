This is Ground - Order Customization

This Is Ground is handmade leather goods that started as creative solutions to everyday problems. Their first products, the Cord Taco and the Cordito, gained attention and love because of their simple design and utility.

Objectives

The goal of this project was to work as a group with a client on a feature that would give users ability to search for a product, customize it with an artwork (ex. logo) and place a bulk order.

My role

Create user interface for bulk orders and order customizations.
